About ObjectVision

An intelligent system powered by Deep Learning & Computer Vision

ObjectVision is a cutting-edge AI-powered web application engineered for real-time object detection, multi-class classification, and detailed analysis of visual data. Built with a focus on precision and usability, it delivers an intuitive, responsive interface that transforms complex AI insights into actionable intelligence.

At its core, ObjectVision harnesses the power of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) layered with ResNet18 architecture. CNNs meticulously extract hierarchical features from images, while ResNet's residual skip connections mitigate vanishing gradients‚Äîenabling deeper networks, superior accuracy (up to 95% on benchmark datasets), and rapid inference times.
