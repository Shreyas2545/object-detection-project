Frequently Asked Questions

What types of images can I upload?

You can upload JPG, PNG, or JPEG images up to 10MB. Use clear, well-lit images with a single prominent object. Models detect persons, animals, vehicles, furniture, electronics, food items, sports equipment, and tools.

How does the comparative analysis work?

Your image is processed through YOLO, CNN, ResNet-18, MobileNet, KNN, SVM, Decision Tree, and Random Forest. Each outputs a confidence score shown in a comparative bar chart.

Which model is the most accurate?

Deep learning models (YOLO, CNN, ResNet-18, MobileNet) achieve the highest accuracy. YOLO typically performs best due to advanced real-time detection.

Can I use the webcam on mobile devices?

Yes ‚Äî works on modern mobile browsers. Allow camera permissions. If unavailable, use file upload instead.

Is my uploaded data stored or shared?

No ‚Äî all processing happens locally in your browser. Images are never stored or uploaded.

What do the accuracy percentages mean?

Accuracy % = model confidence level. Scores above 85% are considered highly reliable.

Why do different models show different accuracies?

Different algorithms ‚Äî DL models learn patterns, ML models rely on statistical methods. Accuracy differences reflect model strengths.

Can I test multiple images in one session?

Yes ‚Äî after viewing results click ‚ÄúTest Another Image‚Äù and repeat as many times as you like.
